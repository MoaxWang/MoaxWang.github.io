# Product

## Moment Definition

Define 0, 1st, and 2nd moment:

$$
\begin{align}
\mu_0&=\sum^\infty_{n=1}{D_n}\\
\mu_1&=\sum^\infty_{n=1}{nD_n}\\
\mu_2&=\sum^\infty_{n=1}{n^2D_n}
\end{align}
$$

Where D<sub>n</sub> is the concentration of dead chain with chain length n in certain moment.
